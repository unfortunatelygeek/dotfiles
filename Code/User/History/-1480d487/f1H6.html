How Lazy 2D Will Work:

  1. Choose an item from our catalog.
  2. Grant camera access.
  3. Upload a clear photo of yourself.
  4. A Stable Viton model will analyze your photo.
  5. See the selected item overlaid realistically on your image.
  6. Adjust sizes, colors, and styles directly!

The Stable Viton model is an advanced neural network designed to simulate how selected clothing items will appear on your body. Developed by pioneers in artificial intelligence and fashion technology, it ensures a highly realistic virtual fitting experience.
